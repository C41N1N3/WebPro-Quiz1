Hometown

Nestled strategically on the east coast of Kalimantan lies Bontang, a vibrant city and a cornerstone of Indonesian industry just 120 km from Samarinda. While home to industrial powerhouses like Badak NGL (natural gas), Pupuk Kalimantan Timur (fertilizer), and Indominco Mandiri (coal), Bontang's identity thrives beyond its factories. It is an ever-expanding hub of commerce and services, bringing energy and new opportunities to its 190,000+ residents on the dynamic edge of the Makassar Strait.
