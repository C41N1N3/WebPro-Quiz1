About

Hello! I'm Akhdan from ITS. The website you're viewing is a project I created for my Web Programming Quiz 1.

For me, building a website is more than just an assignment; it's a canvas for exploration. Through this project, I challenged myself to learn and grow. This site is a reflection of my learning journey—showcasing what I can do now and where my passion will take me next

Brief History

My journey into web development began long before my formal studies. It started back in junior high school when, fueled by a shared dream with my friends, I took my first shot at building a website. The goal was ambitious: to create a platform for us as aspiring content creators.

While our content creator careers didn't quite take off, the experience of creating that simple, single-page website was incredibly exciting. That project ignited my passion for building things on the web and taught me that the process itself can be just as rewarding as the final outcome—a lesson that still drives me today.
